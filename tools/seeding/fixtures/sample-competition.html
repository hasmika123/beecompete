Nova Math Challenge

The national mathematics competition for middle schoolers.

About

The Nova Math Challenge is an annual, individual mathematics competition open to students in grades 6 through 8 across the United States. Students compete online through a proctored exam. There is no cost to participate.

Who can enter

  • Open to students in grades 6–8 (middle school).
  • Students must be enrolled in a U.S. school.
  • Register as an individual — no school sponsor required.

Format

The competition is a 90-minute online exam of 25 short-answer problems covering algebra, number theory, geometry, and combinatorics. Calculators are not permitted. Problems are short-answer, not proof-based.

Key dates

Registration opens September 1 and closes October 15. The exam is held on November 8.
